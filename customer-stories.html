Sentriq Customer Stories

How teams use Sentriq to get audit-ready and stay there. All figures are self-reported by the customers described. (These are illustrative examples.)

Northwind Ledger — SOC 2 Type II in 11 weeks

Industry: Fintech (accounting automation) · Plan: Scale

Northwind Ledger needed a SOC 2 Type II report to close enterprise deals but had no dedicated security hire. They connected AWS, GitHub, Okta, and Google Workspace in their first week.

  • Evidence automation removed roughly 80% of the manual screenshot collection their team had budgeted for.
  • The Auditor Hub let their auditor self-serve evidence, cutting fieldwork email threads to near zero.
  • They reached a clean Type II report 11 weeks after kickoff.

"We treated Sentriq as the system of record for controls. By the time the audit started, the evidence was already there." — VP Engineering

Harbor Health Systems — HIPAA and SOC 2 from one evidence set

Industry: Digital health · Plan: Enterprise

Harbor Health needed HIPAA readiness and a SOC 2 report for the same set of systems. Maintaining two parallel programs by hand was the status quo they wanted to kill.

  • Mapped one evidence set across both frameworks, so connecting an integration once satisfied controls in both.
  • Used data-residency (EU region) hosting to meet a payer partner's requirement.
  • Quarterly access reviews across Okta and Entra ID became a guided, signed-off workflow instead of a spreadsheet.

"The overlap between HIPAA and SOC 2 used to mean double the work. Now it's one program with two outputs." — Head of Compliance

Atlas Robotics — deflecting security questionnaires at scale

Industry: Industrial automation · Plan: Growth

Atlas Robotics was losing days per deal answering inbound security questionnaires.

  • Stood up a public Trust Center on their own domain to share certifications and gated documents under NDA.
  • Built a Knowledge Library of reusable answers; AI-drafted responses cut questionnaire turnaround from days to hours.
  • Routed Critical and High findings to an on-call Slack channel so issues were fixed before the next monitoring cycle.

"Most prospects now answer their own questions from our Trust Center. The ones who don't get a same-day response." — Security Lead
