Data Processing and Subprocessors

This page explains what data Sentriq processes on your behalf, the subprocessors we use to deliver the service, and how to stay informed of changes. It complements (and does not replace) your signed Data Processing Agreement (DPA).

What data Sentriq processes

To monitor your controls and store evidence, Sentriq processes:

  • Configuration and posture data read from your connected systems (for example, encryption settings, MFA status, branch-protection rules). Monitoring integrations are read-only and least-privilege.
  • Identity and access data such as user lists and group membership from your identity provider, used for access reviews.
  • Evidence artifacts you or your integrations generate, plus the control each supports and the collection timestamp.
  • Workspace user data — names and emails of your Sentriq users, for authentication and notifications.

Sentriq does not require write access to your production systems and does not store customer end-user content from your applications.

Data Processing Agreement

A DPA is available to all customers and is incorporated into our standard terms. It covers processing purposes, confidentiality, security measures, sub-processing, international transfer mechanisms, and assistance with data-subject requests. Request a signed copy from your CSM or privacy@sentriq.site.

Subprocessors

Sentriq uses the following categories of subprocessors to operate the service. Specific vendors are disclosed in the current subprocessor list available on request and in your Trust Center.

Purpose Category
Cloud hosting and storage Primary infrastructure provider(s)
Database and search Managed data services within the hosting provider
Transactional email Email delivery for notifications
Error monitoring and logging Operational observability
Customer support tooling In-app chat and ticketing
AI/LLM processing Drafting questionnaire answers and summaries

Data residency

Standard hosting is in major cloud regions. EU-region data residency is available on Enterprise. Contact your CSM to confirm region options before onboarding.

AI processing

When you use AI-assisted features (for example, Knowledge Library answer drafts), the relevant content is processed by an LLM subprocessor under contractual confidentiality terms. Your data is not used to train third-party foundation models. AI features are optional and can be disabled by an admin.

Notification of changes

We maintain a current subprocessor list and notify customers of additions in advance, as described in the DPA. Subscribe to subprocessor change notifications from your Trust Center or by emailing privacy@sentriq.site. Customers may object to a new subprocessor under the terms set out in the DPA.

Data deletion and retention

You can request deletion of your workspace data at any time via support@sentriq.site. On termination, data is deleted per your contract's retention terms. Admins can also remove individual evidence artifacts in-app.

Contact

Privacy and data-processing questions: privacy@sentriq.site. Security questions: see the Trust Center or security@sentriq.site.
