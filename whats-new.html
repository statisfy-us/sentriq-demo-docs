What's New in Sentriq

Product updates, new integrations, and framework support — newest first. Subscribe to the changelog RSS or watch the in-app What's New panel to stay current. Dates are when the change reached general availability.

June 2026

Azure Blob Storage and Entra ID monitoring

Continuous Monitoring now covers Azure Blob Storage encryption and public-access controls, plus conditional-access and MFA posture from Microsoft Entra ID. Connect under Integrations → Cloud.

Auditor Hub comments now thread

Auditor comments on a control now thread, so back-and-forth during fieldwork stays in one place. Replies notify the assigned control owner in Slack and email.

Faster evidence export

Evidence package exports now stream as they build, so large SOC 2 Type II packages (12-month windows) start downloading in seconds instead of waiting for the full archive.

May 2026

ISO 27001:2022 transition complete

All ISO 27001 workspaces are now on the 2022 control set (Annex A, 93 controls). The 2013 mapping remains available read-only for historical audits. No action needed — your existing evidence was re-mapped automatically.

Trust Center custom domains

You can now serve your Trust Center from your own domain (for example, trust.yourcompany.com) with an automatically provisioned TLS certificate. Available on Growth and above.

GitLab integration

GitLab joins GitHub for source-control monitoring: branch-protection rules, required approvals, and secret-scanning status now feed your change-management controls.

April 2026

Severity-based finding routing

Finding alerts can now be routed by severity: send Critical and High to a PagerDuty-style on-call Slack channel while Medium and Low go to a digest. Configure under Settings → Alerts.

Knowledge Library AI drafts

When you receive a security questionnaire, the Knowledge Library now drafts answers from your existing approved responses and your Trust Center content. You review and approve before anything is sent.

Quarterly access reviews

A guided workflow for quarterly user-access reviews: Sentriq pulls current access from Okta, Google Workspace, and Entra ID, routes it to reviewers, and stores the sign-off as evidence.

March 2026

HIPAA module general availability

The HIPAA module is now GA, covering the Security Rule, Privacy Rule basics, and Breach Notification readiness, with a reusable mapping to your existing SOC 2 evidence.

1Password and CrowdStrike integrations

Monitor secrets-management hygiene from 1Password and endpoint-protection coverage from CrowdStrike. Both are read-only and least-privilege.

Slack Connect for Enterprise

Enterprise customers can now open a shared Slack Connect channel with their dedicated CSM directly from the in-app support menu.
